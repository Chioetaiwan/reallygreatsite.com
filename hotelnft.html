What is NFT Stay Ownership?

Healing Home NFT grants you exclusive rights to stay at premium villas each year. It’s a digital asset that proves your right to a yearly stay, powered by blockchain.

How It Works

  1. Purchase: Select and buy your NFT membership.
  2. Own: Your NFT is stored in your wallet as proof of stay rights.
  3. Reveal: Your random stay date is assigned upon purchase.
  4. Access: Three months prior, receive "THE KEY" NFT for check-in.
  5. Enjoy: Use our app for keyless entry and a luxury experience.
